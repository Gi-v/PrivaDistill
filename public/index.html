What is ε-differential privacy?

A model satisfies (ε, δ)-differential privacy if adding or removing any single training record changes the model's output distribution by at most a factor of eε.

Lower ε = stronger privacy guarantee. PrivaDistill enforces this by clipping per-sample gradients and injecting calibrated Gaussian noise during training (DP-SGD).

ε < 1Strong
1 – 10Moderate
> 10Weak
